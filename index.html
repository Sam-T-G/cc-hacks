01 Why MESA

MESA exists for these students.
So does CC Hacks.

MESA supports educationally and financially disadvantaged students to excel in STEM and transfer. A community-college-only hackathon puts those same students in front of a project, a mentor, and a network for a weekend. The audiences are not similar. They are the same.

For your students, that means hands-on experience, a portfolio piece, and exposure to the Google developer community. Each one strengthens a transfer application.

  • For studentsA real project, mentors, and a network in one weekend.
  • For MESAA high-visibility STEM event, organized for you, not by you.
  • For the regionCommunity college talent, finally on a stage built for it.
